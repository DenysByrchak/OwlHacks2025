About NextStopPhilly

Discover Philadelphia like never before — explore the city’s landmarks, activities, and events all in one place.

Our Mission

NextStopPhilly aims to help both visitors and locals easily explore Philadelphia’s rich history, diverse culture, and vibrant attractions. Our platform lets you find nearby landmarks, view upcoming events, and build a personalized schedule.

What You’ll Find

  • Interactive map of attractions and activities
  • Curated events and highlights
  • Personalized scheduling to plan your trips with ease
